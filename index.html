Common Questions

Frequently Asked Queries

Everything you need to know about photo guidelines, shipping options, and secure payment setups.

📸 Does my reference photo need to be professional quality?
＋
Not at all! Standard phone snapshots, casual selfies, or group photos work perfectly. Our artists analyze details with extreme care. We can even merge characters from separate individual photos into a single, beautifully unified composition portrait! Just ensure that facial features are clearly visible under decent lighting so we can render them accurately.
🚀 What are your shipping timelines and framing options?
＋
We offer Free Express Delivery across India! Standard orders take 3 to 5 business days from your final draft confirmation on WhatsApp to reach your doorstep. Every portrait is printed on art-grade archives, housed inside shock-proof premium frames, and wrapped in thick luxury bubble protection to guarantee zero transit damage.
🛡️ How secure is the payment flow? Do I pay upfront?
＋
Your confidence is our priority! You do not have to complete payment before photo verification. You select sizes and upload references first; then our studio artist contacts you on WhatsApp to configure alignment. Once you give 100% approval on the digital proof sketch, we drop an encrypted, highly secure payment interface link or UPI QR code in the chat.
✍️ Can I add customized lines, dates, or messages to the design?
＋
Absolutely! Personalizing your art makes it unforgettable. You can specify names, custom dates, or a emotional message/quote during product selection or reference upload. If you forget, simply let our support artist know during your WhatsApp consultation, and we will artistically position your text onto the final composition free of charge!
